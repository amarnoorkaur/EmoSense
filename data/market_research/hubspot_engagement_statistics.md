# Social Media Engagement Statistics (HubSpot Data)

## Data from HubSpot's State of Marketing Reports (Free Public Data)

### Executive Summary
This document compiles freely available statistics from HubSpot's annual State of Marketing reports, focusing on social media engagement patterns and content performance. All data is publicly accessible through HubSpot's website.

---

## Content Performance by Emotion (HubSpot Blog Research)

### High-Performing Emotion Types
Based on HubSpot's analysis of millions of social media posts:

**Positive Emotions:**
- Posts evoking **joy** get 2.3x more engagement than neutral posts
- **Inspirational content** receives 1.8x more shares
- **Humor-based posts** generate 2.1x more comments

**Negative Emotions (Constructive):**
- Posts addressing **pain points** get 1.7x more clicks
- **Problem-solving content** receives 2.5x more saves
- **Educational + empathetic** content drives 1.9x more conversions
